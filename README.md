# 📊 SunCoast Retail Visual Analysis

A comprehensive **data visualization and analytics project** using Python, focused on retail sales performance, customer behavior, and marketing efficiency.

This project simulates real-world retail data and applies visualization techniques to generate actionable business insights.

---

## 📁 Project Overview

This project analyzes:

* 📈 Quarterly sales trends
* 🏬 Location-based performance
* 🛍️ Product category performance
* 📣 Advertising effectiveness
* 👥 Customer demographics
* 💰 Purchase behavior and pricing tiers

All data is **synthetically generated** using reproducible random processes.

## 📊 Features & Visualizations

### 1. Time Series Analysis

* Quarterly sales trends
* Sales comparison across locations

### 2. Categorical Analysis

* Category performance by location (grouped bar chart)
* Sales composition (stacked bar chart)

### 3. Relationship Analysis

* Advertising spend vs. sales (scatter plot)
* Advertising efficiency over time

### 4. Customer Insights

* Age distribution (overall + by location)
* Purchase behavior by age group (box plot)

### 5. Sales Distribution

* Purchase amount histogram
* Sales breakdown by pricing tier (pie chart)

### 6. Market Share Analysis

* Category market share
* Location-based sales distribution

### 7. Business Dashboard

A combined visualization including:

* Sales trends
* Location performance
* Market share
* Advertising impact

## 🏗️ Data Generation

The dataset is **fully synthetic** and includes:

### Sales Data

* 8 quarters (2022–2023)
* 4 locations:

  * Tampa
  * Miami
  * Orlando
  * Jacksonville
* 5 product categories:

  * Electronics
  * Clothing
  * Home Goods
  * Sporting Goods
  * Beauty

### Customer Data

* 2,000 simulated customers
* Age-based purchasing behavior
* Price tiers:

  * Budget
  * Mid-range
  * Premium
